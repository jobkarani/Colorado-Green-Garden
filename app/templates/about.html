Why Choose us

Healthy Living

Welcome to Bonjoe Electronics, your number one source for all things Electronics. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of electronics, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness.

Founded in 2022 by Josiah and Boniface, Healthy Living Electronics has come a long way from its beginnings in Nairobi, gaborone rd on kamae rd, Frontline building. When Josiah and Boniface first started out, their passion for providing quality, durable and affordable products to the foot of the consumers drove them to do intense research and gave them the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Kenya, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly and fair trade wing of the Ecommerce industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
